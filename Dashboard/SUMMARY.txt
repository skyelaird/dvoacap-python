HOW THE PROPAGATION MODEL WORKS
================================

The system uses sophisticated ITU-R based calculations:

1. MUF Calculation
   - Critical frequency (foF2) from solar flux
   - Path geometry (single vs multi-hop)
   - Distance and bearing to target
   - Time of day (diurnal variation)

2. Signal Quality Assessment
   - Frequency vs MUF ratio (optimal: 85% of MUF)
   - Reliability percentage
   - SNR estimation in dB
   - Absorption factors

3. Geomagnetic Effects
   - Kp index impact on reliability
   - A-index for absorption
   - Aurora warnings

4. Gray Line Enhancement
   - Better propagation at sunrise/sunset
   - Band-specific adjustments

This is MORE sophisticated than "basic" but simplified compared to
full VOACAP. Good enough for practical operating decisions!
