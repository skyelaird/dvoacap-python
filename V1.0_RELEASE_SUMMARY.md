### The Solution (After)
```
MIT License

Copyright (c) 2025 Joel Morin and Contributors

This software is based on DVOACAP by Alex Shovkoplyas (VE3NEA)
https://github.com/VE3NEA/DVOACAP
Copyright (c) 2025 Alex Shovkoplyas

DVOACAP is based on VOACAP algorithms developed by Voice of America
and the Institute for Telecommunication Sciences (ITS).
Original VOACAP software is in the public domain.
```

1. **DVOACAP was relicensed** from MPL 1.1 to MIT in May 2025 by Alex Shovkoplyas
2. **dvoacap-python was created** in November 2025 AFTER the relicensing
3. **MIT permits derivative works** under any license
4. **Proper attribution** maintained throughout (VOACAP ‚Üí DVOACAP ‚Üí dvoacap-python)
